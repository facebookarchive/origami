Long Press

Lets you get a long press interaction. A long press is a press that’s held for a given duration without being moved.

Long Press

  • Enable
  • Duration
  • Press
Enable

When this is off, the patch won’t respond to presses.

Duration

The amount of time in seconds the touch needs to be down for before it’s considered a long press.

Press

Outputs a 1 once your finger has been down for the specified duration and remains a 1 until you release your finger.
