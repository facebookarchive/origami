We believe the right tools can empower designers to explore new ideas and create more innovative and effective work. Origami is just the beginning of what we want to do here. Come join our team to imagine and build the design tools of the future.

Software Engineer — Design Tools

The Design Tools team is looking for a unique individual who combines a strong technical background with a deep understanding of the design and implementation of modern gestural interfaces.

Our team builds tools for interaction designers used within Facebook and across the industry. Our job is to provide software that helps them express and refine their ideas into products that will be used daily by hundreds of millions of people.

You will have the opportunity to work across our interaction prototyping tool Origami (http://origami.facebook.com), plugins to popular design apps, and our internal design sharing tools. Working closely with designers across the company, you will implement refinements to the UI design process, from initial sketches to implementation.

Experience with Apple platforms is a must, though your adventures may take you across Mac, iOS, Android and Web or backend APIs. Someone with an eye towards emerging ideas in Swift, c++, and other languages will thrive on our team.

If you are interested in working hard on a small and ambitious team within a strong engineering and design organization, we would love to hear from you!

Responsibilities

  • Work on future versions of Origami to improve efficiency and learnability
  • Build custom user interfaces for OS X
  • Debug complex issues reported internally and externally
  • Answer questions from designers using our tools
  • Analyze and optimize code for performance
  • Work on new tools and plugins for existing software

Requirements

  • 4+ years of software development experience
  • 2+ years building complex applications for the iOS or Mac using Objective-C/C++
  • Ability to understand and debug large and complex code bases
  • History of learning new technologies and approaches proactively
  • Experience with multithreading programming / gcd
  • Experience writing unit tests and testable code
  • Knowledge of Mac or iOS SDK performance tools and optimization techniques
  • Excellent problem solving, critical thinking and communication skills

Applicable Skills

  • Experience with writing modern C++11
  • Depth of experience using AppKit on OS X
  • Familiarity with programming langugage design and implementation
  • Experience working collaboratively with designers
  • Knowledge of Origami / Quartz composer
  • A background in computer graphics and math

Design Program Manager

The Design Program Manager (DPM) for Design Tools role is a relatively new role at Facebook. DPMs establish, support and refine operational systems and tools that enable our designers to do what they do best...design our products. This particular DPM opportunity is focused on managing the growth and development of Facebook's design tools that help our product designers design, share, and execute more effectively, including new features for Origami (https://facebook.github.io/origami/) as well as other internal tools.

The DPM will project manage a nimble cross functional team to establish a prioritized feature list based on team feedback and lead ongoing design and development cycles. Additionally, the DPM will be responsible for developing educational and training programs for these tools, evangelizing updates both internally and externally, and continually soliciting feedback for improvements. Facebook is a fast moving company that requires a DPM with great interpersonal skills to develop relationships across the company and find opportunities to help our talented teams operate at their highest level. The DPM role requires broad knowledge of design and development processes, current visual design and prototyping tools, excellent communication skills, and project management skills. This is a full-time position based in our Menlo Park office.

Responsibilities

  • Work with teams to prioritize feature lists and execute across multiple design tools initiatives
  • Project manage engineering and design team members to track activity on projects and establish a clear path forward
  • Establish communication structure across projects
  • Develop and run educational programs for designers using tools
  • Collect feedback, measure success, and make improvements to toolsets
  • Own understanding how projects are delivering on expectations and be on point for tracking reviews and ensuring issues are addressed and aligned with goals
  • Facilitate relationships between teams, moving process forward, finding resources, reworking schedules, raising concerns to appropriate owners early and resolving open issues that prevent progress
  • Predict and/or identify roadblocks and obstacles and establish meetings or integration points between teams to resolve them
  • Prioritize competing needs with good reasoning and creative solutions
  • Make connections, remove barriers, and move things forward

Requirements

  • Experience working with designers and engineers to design, build, and deliver products
  • Familiarity with Origami, Facebook's prototyping tool as well as other design tools in the market (Photoshop, Sketch, Pixate, Framer, etc.)
  • Thoughtful, persistent (yet friendly and collaborative) personality
  • General knowledge of design/UX and product development
  • Strong track record of operating independently, demonstrating creativity, being detail-oriented, and delivering results in a highly organized manner
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills
  • 4+ years of relevant or related working experience
  • Experience establishing and leading training sessions or workshops a plus
